 Alhamdulillah, all the praises and thanks be to Almighty Allah who created the heavens and earth for a true purpose. iErTA the acronym of Islamic Education, Research and Tabligh Academy , an international organisation of Da'wah and Tabligh. Do you know what is Dawat E Tablig yet? Dawat means 'call', Tabligh means to reach. People are called to the unique ideals of Islam. Allah Subahanahu Wa Ta'ala says: "Call to the way of your Lord (Allah), with wisdom and good advice." (Sura-16 Nahl, verse: 125). Tabligh is the message of welfare of Islam delivered to people's door. In Hadith Sharif, Rasulullah Sallallahu Alaihi Wasallam said: “Deliver even one message from me.” (Muslim). The final words of the Holy Prophet Muhammad Sallallahu Alaihi Wasallam in the Farewell Hajj speech were, "You who are present, convey to those who are absent" (Al Bidayah Wan Nihaya). Dawat Tabligh command good deeds and forbid evil deeds. Allah Subhanahu Wa Ta'ala says, 'You are the best Ummah, you have been brought forth for the welfare of mankind; Enjoin good deeds, forbid evil deeds and believe in Allah.' (Surah Ale Imran, verse: 110). To know more click here, www.ierta.org
