About Us

iErTA

Islamic Education, Research and Tabligh Academy

Alhamdulillah, all the praises and thanks be to Allah Subhanahu Wa Ta'ala. iErTA is the acronym of Islamic Education, Research and Tabligh Academy. It's an international organisation of Da'wah and Tabligh, founded by Former Cheif Executive Officer (CEO) Muhammad Erag Goshih.

What we do, specially we are working worldwide in the field of Qur'an and Hadith, Da'wat E Tabligh, Islamic Education & Research, The New-Muslims Outreach, Da'wah in Muslims and Non-Muslims, Help to built Educational Projects and Institutions, Medical and Health Treatment as well as a single person according to the Islamic Shari'ah, insha'Allah.

The main motto of iErTA - Islamic Education, Research and Tabligh Academy is "Be coloured by the color of Almighty Allah" [Surah Bakharah: 138]
